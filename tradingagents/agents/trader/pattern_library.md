## 1. Buying Climax Short (顶部高潮空)
**Source**: Kyle Williams ($7.8M verified, 55.7% WR)
**Type**: Mean-reversion short on parabolic blow-off tops
**Edge**: A+ setup, rare but high conviction

### Conditions (ALL required):
- 3+ consecutive green daily candles with total gain ≥ 20% (BTC-adjusted)
- Each day's gain accelerating (bigger than previous)
- Volume increasing daily, climax day = extreme/record volume
- Retail FOMO maxed (F&G ≥ 80, extreme social sentiment)
- No significant pullback during the run (pure momentum)

### Execution:
- **Panic line**: Previous daily close — where retail capitulates
- **Defense line**: First rejection high after initial drop (HARD stop, no exceptions)
- **Entry**: Small probe near defense line on weakness + add on break below panic line
- **TP1**: 10% of the day's range, scale out remainder at key levels
- **Hold thesis**: Overextended assets correct for days after blow-off tops

### Key Principles:
- Small position size due to extreme volatility
- Hard stop above defense line — if price makes new high, bubble continues, get out
- This is a MOMENTUM EXHAUSTION play, not a top-pick
- In BTC: watch funding rate spikes, exchange volume records, social media FOMO metrics
